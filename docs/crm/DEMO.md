# Demo Guide

Use these two journeys for a short team walkthrough.

## Demo 1: Kampong Lead Risk Review

Link:

```text
https://adspaceengineeer.github.io/frailty-coach-CRM/crm.html#journey-kampong-lead
```

Talk track:

1. "This is the kampong-level view: not one elder, but the whole care network."
2. Point to **Enrolled residents**, **Average function score**, **Follow-up queue**, and **Reassessment due**.
3. The registry filters to follow-up residents.
4. The page moves to **Providers**, showing care-team load.
5. Close with: "A kampong lead can spot risk and redistribute follow-up."

Buttons to click:

- **Demo 1**
- **Population**
- **Providers**
- **Copy briefing**

## Demo 2: Care Coordinator Outreach

Link:

```text
https://adspaceengineeer.github.io/frailty-coach-CRM/crm.html#journey-outreach
```

Talk track:

1. "This is the daily work queue for a care coordinator."
2. Review each resident and the suggested next action.
3. Click **Log action**.
4. Open **Interventions**.
5. Close with: "Frailty signals become operational follow-up, not just dashboard numbers."

Buttons to click:

- **Demo 2**
- **Outreach**
- **Log action**
- **Interventions**
