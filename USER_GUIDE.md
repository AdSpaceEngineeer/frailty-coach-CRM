# User Guide

Frailty Coach - Kampong is designed for care-network users such as care coordinators, clinic leads, community programme managers, and hospital group teams.

## Population

Use **Population** to review the overall resident cohort.

- Top cards show enrolled residents, average function score, follow-up queue, and reassessment due.
- The trend panel shows four-week function score movement.
- The priority queue highlights residents needing attention.
- Registry filters show all residents, follow-up residents, reassessment due residents, or low-adherence residents.

## Outreach

Use **Outreach** for the daily contact queue.

Each card names a resident and suggests a next action, such as caregiver contact, workout nudge, or reassessment scheduling.

Click **Log action** to show the intended intervention workflow.

## Providers

Use **Providers** to review care-team load.

Each card shows:

- care team or provider group
- lead person
- resident count
- average score
- follow-up count

## Interventions

Use **Interventions** to show the action-tracking concept.

The current prototype includes seeded intervention examples. A production version would connect this to a case-management database.

## Export

Click **Export CSV** to download a simple resident cohort file.

Click **Copy briefing** to copy a short operational summary.
